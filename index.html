Sigma Psi Zeta Sorority, Inc.

Sigma Psi Zeta Sorority, Inc. is a progressive, multicultural Greek Organization that provides a means to success and support for womxn, particularly womxn of color. There are 35 active memberships across the United States. I am President of the Kappa Charter membership! We are super involved in the multicultural Greek community and often partner with the New River Valley Women's Resource Center for philanthropy and service events.
